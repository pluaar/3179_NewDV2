Malaysia's International Arrivals Across Different States From 2020-2023

Total Arrivals by State in Malaysia (2020-2023)

This map visualizes the total number of international arrivals in Malaysia from 2020 to 2023, across different states.

Kuala Lumpur (KL) has no direct entry points, hence there is no data available for KL.


Areas with darker shades indicate higher arrivals.

Selangor and Johor are consistently the busiest entry points.

Percentage of Arrivals by Gender for Each State in Malaysia (2020-2023)

This pyramid bar chart represents the gender distribution of arrivals by state, highlighting differences in male and female visitor numbers.

States like Sabah and Pulau Pinang show a balanced gender ratio among visitors.

Percentage of Arrivals by Continent for Each State in Malaysia (2020-2023)

This stacked bar chart shows the percentage of arrivals by continent for each state, allowing for a comparison of geographic sources of visitors.

Asia dominates in terms of arrivals across all states.

Monthly Arrivals for Each State in Year 2023

Heatmap depicting monthly arrivals in 2023, with color intensity reflecting the volume of arrivals.

December and January typically see peak arrivals due to holiday seasons.

Monthly Arrivals for Each State in Year 2022

Heatmap of monthly arrivals in 2022.

There is a significant increase during the mid-year festival seasons.

Monthly Arrivals for Each State in Year 2021

2021's arrivals heatmap shows trends amidst global travel restrictions.

The recovery phase in travel can be observed in the later months.

Monthly Arrivals for Each State in Year 2020

Early 2020 saw normal travel patterns before the onset of global travel restrictions.
